Purpose

The purpose of this activity is to design a simple engineering product based on requirements of a client. We were tasked with creating a model for a unique duplo brick that can connect to other duplos, has some form of writing on it, and can be no larger than 8 cubic inches.

  • Used Uncertainty and Significant Figures to record, report and evaluate measurements
  • Use Engineering Design concepts to design a custom Duplo - Design Space, Constraints, Criteria, Divergent Thinking (Brainstorming) and Convergent Thinking (Card Sorting)
  • Use Engineering Graphics to model, visualize, and 3D print the custom Duplo

Description of the product

The product is a 3D printed Duplo piece which can be connected on top of other duplo pieces. The product is designed with contemporary mindset and following the trend of 'Among Us' game and is developed to work with existing Duplo pieces so that the client can use the product without actually buying a whole new set of Duplos.

Because of our design, we do not have a male connector, and instead only have a female connector on the bottom of the brick.
